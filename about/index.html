About

Founded by Joshua Herron, Sophie Border and Joseph Madril in 2020, STiBaRC Records is an independent record label primarily focused on the alternative/indie genre. The STiBaRC Records discography also has releases in the soundtrack genre, pop and country.

If you haven't yet, check out our Spotify page for playlists and follow our STiBaRC page for updates!
